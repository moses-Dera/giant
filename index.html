Introduction

Your privacy is important to us at Giant Strides Online School. We respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, virtual learning platforms, and portal.

Giant Strides Online School (“Giant Strides”) operates the giantstridesonlineschool.com website (the “Website”) and the associated student/parent learning portals (the “Platform”) to offer online education, virtual classrooms, and related academic services. By using any Giant Strides services, you accept this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your Personal Information as described below.

Information We Collect and Why

To enroll a student and set up portal access to our virtual learning tools, we collect only the necessary information to facilitate academic excellence.

Parent / Guardian Information

  • Full Name (for legal records and identity verification)
  • Email Address (for portal setup, updates, and communication)
  • Phone Number (for emergency and support verification)
  • Billing Details (processed securely for tuition payments)

Student Information

  • Full Name (for academic registration and grading ledgers)
  • Date of Birth (to ensure age-appropriate grade placement)
  • Grade Level (for class curriculum assignment)

This minimal data helps us create a unique Student Identification Number (SID) for your child’s account, which we use to securely manage academic grades, progress reports, and virtual classroom access. For school administration, tuition processing, and identity verification, we collect additional verification details as required to comply with local educational standards and safely configure payment portals for secure automated billing.

Minimal Data Collection & Safety

Prioritizing Children's Safety & Privacy

As an online school, we strictly respect and prioritize children's safety and privacy. We design our online classrooms to protect students’ privacy and limit data exposure.

What We Do NOT Collect

Unnecessary Details

We do not collect unnecessary personal details, private contact info, or physical home addresses of children, unless legally required for regulatory school records.

Media & Likeness

We do not collect or use your child's image or likeness for public or commercial use without explicit, separate written parental consent.

Our virtual portal is structured so that student accounts are tied securely to academic records via their SID, keeping their broader personal identity safe and secure from public exposure.

Security and Data Protection

Giant Strides Online School employs industry-standard encryption protocols and stores all parents' files, student portfolios, and communication data on secure, encrypted servers. We ensure that academic records, live-stream classroom streams, and tuition transactions are fully protected.

Automatic Data Collection

Like many digital education platforms, we automatically collect certain technical information when you navigate our site to ensure the security and reliability of our services.

IP Addresses

To detect and prevent unauthorized access and verify logging locations.

Browser Types

To optimize rendering, portal layout, and ensure compatibility with classroom tools.

Device Information

To adapt our interactive dashboard and virtual feeds to your screen and OS.

Usage Statistics

To monitor stream latency, connection drops, and improve platform responsiveness.

This technical metadata is stored securely and is used solely to enhance system performance, monitor virtual classroom stability, and prevent unauthorized portal access.

Privacy and Anonymity Measures

Student ID (SID) System

Each student's progress, grading ledger, and profile are linked primarily to their unique SID. This limits the exposure of full names and personal information during day-to-day interactions across digital school modules.

Secure Virtual Classrooms

Access to our live classes and discussion forums is strictly password-protected and restricted to registered students and verified faculty. We do not allow external access under any circumstances.

Your Choices & Rights

As a parent or guardian, you maintain complete sovereignty over your family's personal data. We provide direct mechanisms to exercise these rights.

You can request to inspect, update, or correct any personal details associated with your guardian profile or your child's student record at any time. Updates can also be made directly within the Parent Portal dashboard.

You may opt out of receiving marketing newsletters or promotional campaigns. Please note that critical academic alerts, class schedules, grading notifications, and administrative updates remain essential and mandatory for active students.

You can request the complete deletion of your account and your child's personal data from our servers. Note that this right is subject to local academic record retention laws and the completion of active tuition billing cycles.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our dedicated data administrative team at hello@giantstridesonlineschool.com.

Any changes will be published directly on this page with an updated revision date.
